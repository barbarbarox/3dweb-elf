The Prophecy

In the twilight realm of Aetheria, an ancient prophecy foretold the rise of an elven warrior — born under the twin moons, destined to wield a blade forged from starlight and shadow. For centuries, the elves waited.

The Awakening

When darkness crept across the Silverwood, the magic sword Lunaris began to glow for the first time in a thousand years. It called out — not with sound, but with a pulse of pure arcane energy that resonated through the soul of its chosen bearer.

🧝‍♀️

The Elf Warrior

Elysia, last of the Starborne, answered the call. With silver hair and eyes like molten amethyst, she drew the blade from its crystal sheath. The forest erupted in light. The age of reclamation had begun.
